Ingredients:
1 chicken 
1 onion
1 garlic
500 gr of butter 
1 tablespoon of salt 
1/2 tablespoon of black pepper 
1 orange 


Steps 
1. Extract the orange juice and mix with the  onion and the garlic on a cooking pot
2. Add the salt and teh pepper and let stand for an hour
3. Put the chicken inside the pot for two hours
4. Spread the butter in a pyrex and warm the oven tho 275 Celsius degrees
5. Pour the content of the pot into the pyrex
6. Bake for two hours
7. Enjoy
